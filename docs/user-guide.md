# User Guide

## Posting a Mishearing

The post form is a step-by-step wizard:

1. **Paste a video URL** (YouTube, Niconico, SoundCloud, or any URL) ŌĆö the video preview loads immediately. Before pasting, YouTube/Niconico/SoundCloud links are shown below the input for quick access. URLs with time parameters (`?t=`, `?start=`, `?from=`) auto-set the cue start time
2. **Song info**: Artist name, song title, and era are auto-filled from the video title via oEmbed (you can correct them). Select the original language and "sounds like" language
3. **Subtitle cues**: Use the **dual-thumb slider** to set the start and end of the misheard segment. Type what you hear. Use the **ŌŚĆ Ō¢Č buttons** for 1-second fine-tuning, or **drag the thumb** to seek the video in real-time
   - To add **multiple subtitles** (up to 3) in one post, tap the **+** button. The 2nd cue onwards only needs an end time (start = previous cue's end)
4. **Tags**: Choose up to **3 tags** from 10 genre categories (anime, game, vocaloid, movie, drama, cm, rock, pop, hiphop, metal) to help others find your post
5. **About you**: Nickname, delete key, and a one-liner comment (optional fields are styled differently so you know they're skippable)
6. Hit **Post**!

## Drafts

- Tap **Save draft** to save your work in the browser
- Reopen the **Post** tab later and load it from **Drafts**

## Playing

- Tap the **play button** on a post
- YouTube, Niconico, and SoundCloud videos play inline
- Each subtitle cue appears as a **karaoke-style subtitle** (color sweeps left to right) when its time arrives
- Posts with multiple cues show each subtitle in sequence during playback

## Spoiler

- The misheard text is **hidden by default** ŌĆö you see the song title, artist name (era), and video
- It reveals when you **play the video** ŌĆö playback is the only way to see the mishearing
- Below the video: language pair (e.g. enŌåÆja) shown after artist name and era, then meta line with post ID (copyable), date (ISO format), and poster nickname
- **Play count** is displayed with a headphones icon and count
- This way you can listen first and see if you hear it too!

## Reactions

- Tap the **+reaction** label (or **+** button if reactions already exist) to open the emoji picker
- Choose ONE emoji from 12 options per post ŌĆö your reaction
- Tap a different emoji to switch, or tap your current emoji to remove it
- Reactions are shown as emoji badges with counts (like Slack)

## Pickup Corner

- A **jump link** at the top lets you skip straight to the latest posts
- Featured picks appear below the jump link, looking just like regular post cards
- The **master** introduces each song ("First up..." for the 1st, "Next up..." for the rest) ŌåÆ **play the video** ŌåÆ when playback reaches the cue segment, the misheard text and banter automatically appear
- No separate reveal button ŌĆö playback triggers the reveal automatically
- Pickup dates show an "updated" label
- A gradient divider separates the pickup corner from new posts
- Tap **Past picks** for archives

## Hall of Fame

- The **Hall of Fame** tab shows posts ranked by total reactions
- Rank numbers are displayed prominently (large bold text) next to each post
- Top emoji are displayed next to each ranking

## Sharing

- Tap **Share** on any post to copy the URL (or use native share)
- When shared to social media, the post shows a preview with the song info
- Opening the link scrolls to that post

## Pagination

- All tabs use **range-button pagination** (1-, 11-, 21-...) at the top and bottom of the list
- Shows the current page plus one page before and after, with first/last page and **...** ellipsis for gaps

## Header

- When you scroll down, the header shrinks to a **compact bar** showing the site name
- Click/tap the title to scroll back to the top
- Tab navigation stays visible at all times

## Deleting a Post

- Set a **delete key** when posting to enable future deletion
- Without a delete key, posts cannot be removed
